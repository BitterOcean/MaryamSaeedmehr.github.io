Description

Covid-19 was first reported in November 2019 in Wuhan, China. A month later, the World Health Organization (WHO) announced that the virus appeared with clinical symptoms of cough, fever, and pneumonia. On March 11, 2020, when the number of confirmed cases increased, WHO declared COVID-19 a pandemic.
Recently, many studies have been conducted on lung CT images for the early diagnosis of lung diseases as an important sign for the diagnosis of the corona virus, so that the virus can be detected by image processing techniques and without the intervention of a radiologist. The need for early diagnosis and screening in order to accelerate the treatment of this disease has led to the use of convolutional neural network (CNN) as a branch of deep learning techniques.
In this project, I tried to have a relatively complete study for the detection of Covid-19 from CT images using CNN and DenseNet121 architecture.
